"Off the Board" (OTB) is a term used in sports betting when a sportsbook completely removes a game or event from the betting board — meaning no more bets are accepted on that game. This typically happens when significant news drops that would drastically shift the line: a star quarterback's injury status is unknown, breaking news about a team, or a bet the book isn't comfortable taking anymore. The community named itself after this concept because it operates with the same intelligence that causes books to go OTB — staying ahead of the market with sharp-side information before it's reflected in the public line.

Off The Board is a collective group of highly skilled professional handicappers — not a single-person operation.
